# Code of Conduct

Dure is an early open-source project. We want technical disagreement to stay clear, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but correct unsafe designs directly.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Treat security, privacy, and bug bounty topics with extra care.
- Redact secrets, tokens, cookies, private reports, and real user data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or threats.
- Publishing private information or sensitive security material.
- Encouraging unauthorized testing, exploitation, disruption, or data access.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code. Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

For sensitive issues, use a private GitHub security advisory instead of a public issue.
